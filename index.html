Each day you'll be offered one of the five conditions of secure attachment to explore — Safety, Attunement, Soothing, Expressed Delight, or Exploration. You'll receive a quote from the book, a reflection prompt, and a space to write.

There is no right answer. The AI companion that responds to your check-in is not a therapist — it's a reflective presence designed to help you go one step deeper. Your journal stays on your device.
